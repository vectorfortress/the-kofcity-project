How KofCity works

A simple end-to-end order flow from checkout to delivery confirmation.

1
Customer places an order.
The customer selects meals, confirms the delivery address, and pays online or chooses cash on delivery.
2
Vendor receives and prepares.
The vendor accepts the order, prepares the meal, and marks it ready for pickup.
3
Rider picks up and delivers.
An available rider accepts the delivery, picks up the order, and updates the status along the way.
4
Customer or school confirms receipt.
The order is marked delivered, cash is logged when needed, and vendor payout is tracked by admin.
