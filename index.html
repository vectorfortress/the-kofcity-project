How the demo works

Use it like a lightweight prototype before connecting a real backend, Paystack, SMS, and user authentication.

1. Order

Parents or customers choose meals, select a payment method, and submit an order for home or school delivery.

2. Prepare

Vendors receive the order, accept it, and mark it as preparing or ready for pickup.

3. Deliver

Admins assign riders, riders accept delivery, update status, and mark orders delivered.
